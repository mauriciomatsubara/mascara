== Description ==

**Utilize the power of referral marketing to supercharge your sales and grow your business.**

Using this plugin, created by Viral Loops, you can reward your superfans for referring customers to your business. Create and configure your referral campaign in Viral Loops, install the plugin and you're ready to #goViral!

**Simple rewarding**
Offer discounts to your superfans and their referred friends in the form of coupon codes for your e-Commerce store. Two types of discounts:

* Fixed amount off (eg. $10 off)
* Percentage off (eg. 20% off)

Do you want further customizations? [Contact us!](mail:support@viral-loops.com)

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin directly through the WordPress plugins directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Go to [Viral Loops](https://www.viral-loops.com). Create an account if you don't have one already.
4. Create a campaign using the "e-Commerce Referral" template.
5. Configure the campaign and set up rewards for your referrers and their referred friends.
6. Copy the campaign ID and the campaign API key.
7. Back to your WordPress website, edit the *Viral Loops for WooCommerce* settings and add your campaign ID and API key.
8. Ready! Visit your website to see your new referral widget.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What kind of rewards can I offer? =

The rewards offered to referrers and invitees are coupon codes for your e-Commerce store. The coupon codes can be offers for fixed amounts (eg. $10 off) or for percentage discounts (eg. 10% off). Edit your campaign in Viral Loops to set these rewards.

= Do I need WooCommerce to make this work? =

Absolutely.

= Can I offer cash payouts as rewards? =

Unfortunately the rewards can only be coupon codes for your own e-Commerce store.
